# iPhone 

Loop will run on on any iPhone that is running iOS 11.1 or later.

* iPhone X

* iPhone 8/8+

* iPhone 7/7s+

* iPhone 6/6+/6s/6s+/SE

* iPhone 5s 

Where can you find your iPhone’s iOS version?  Go to your iPhone’s Settings>General>About.  The iOS will be listed under “Version”
